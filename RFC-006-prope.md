# RFC-006: PRoPE Camera Conditioning
Projective RoPE from Li et al. (2025). Encodes relative camera geometry as camera-dependent linear transformations in Q/K/V rotations (not additive signals like Plücker). Per-frame projective conditioning integrated into attention operator. For temporally-compressed latents (4 frames → 1 latent frame), uses k-indexed transforms to maintain per-frame conditioning. Keeps PRoPE interface unchanged at the attention operator level. Implementation: compute_rotations(poses, intrinsics) → per-frame rotation tensors, apply(qk, rotations) → modified Q/K.
